A free-to-play first-person character-based tactical shooter

Valorant is a team-based
first-person hero shooter
set in the near future.
Players play as one of a set of Agents,
characters based on several countries
and cultures around the world.

Valorant masters copenhagen:
everything you
need to know! Blend your style and experience on a global,
competitive stage.
You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side
using sharp gunplay and tactical abilities.
And, with one life per-round, you'll need to
think faster than your opponent if you want to survive.
Take on foes across Competitive and Unranked modes as well as Deathmatch and Spike Rush.
